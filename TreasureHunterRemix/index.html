Treasure Hunter

  • High Concept: Scavenge the depths of an ancient crypt to find priceless gems while avoiding deadly gems in near-complete darkness as fast as you can!
  • Theme: Person who glows in the dark
  • Mandated Variety: Keyboard input, the gems spawn completely randomly, and the player is an explorer
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should have fun trying to get the jewels as fast as they can and compete with their friends for the fastest time
  • Gameplay: Controls: MOVE WITH THE ARROW KEYS.
    Objective: Collect 3 white jewels as quickly as possible. Avoid the red jewels as 3 of those will kill you. Green jewel makes your field of vision bigger, purple jewel makes your field of vision smaller
  • Prototype Goal: Limited field of view, "smart" random spawn generator
  • Features: The player's field of view is dynamic with different jewels, and the random spawns of the jewels makes every game different.
  • Setting/Premise: Game is abstract
  • Story: No story.
  • Target Audience: Any age group that can understand the rules can play
  • Play Time: Depends on the player. Can take 30 seconds or several minutes dependong on how careful the player is.
  • Strategies: Find a good balance of moving quickly to minimize time spend while also not moving so fast that you carelessly hit negative jewels and lose.
