Asteroid Outlaw

  • High Concept: Protect the earth against a deadly storm of asteroids as the only space outlaw who can!
  • Theme: Major theme of the game is protecting the world
  • Mandated Variety: The game is a basic shoot'em up.
  • Player Experience Goals: The goal is to have fun while also honing in your skills and reflexes at accurately hitting the asteroids
  • Gameplay: The gameplay within the protoype is fairly simple. The cowboy moves up and down and can shoot at the incoming, randomly generated asteroids. The controls are as follows:
    • Move up: Up arrow key
    • Move down: Down Arrow key
    • Shoot: Space
    Outside this prototype, there will be far more expansive features like multiple weapons, multiple levels.
